About Crystal Montessori School

Crystal Montessori School was founded with a clear vision: to provide quality, child-centered education that builds academic excellence and strong moral values.

Our Story

Crystal Montessori School was founded to provide quality, child-centered education that develops both academic excellence and strong moral character. From its early beginnings, the school set out to meet the need for a learning environment that combines Montessori principles with structured academic growth.

The school's guiding idea was simple: create a safe, supportive, and effective place where every child can learn, grow, explore, and succeed. Beginning with a small number of learners, the school focused on quality teaching, discipline, and personal attention for every child.

As the school grew, it continued to improve through feedback from parents and the wider community. Crystal Montessori School expanded its programs, facilities, and teaching methods, while introducing modern learning tools, ICT education, and co-curricular activities to support the full development of each learner.

Over the years, the school has achieved strong results in the Basic Education Certificate Examination (B.E.C.E.). Learners have performed very well, earning high pass rates and distinctions in core subjects. Many graduates have gained admission to respected senior high schools, showing the strength of the academic foundation built at Crystal Montessori School.

Today, Crystal Montessori School continues to grow as an institution devoted to excellence, innovation, and character building. It remains committed to raising confident, responsible, and successful learners who are prepared for the future.

Our Mission

To nurture God-fearing, intellectually capable, and morally upright learners through a holistic, Montessori-inspired education that prepares them for excellence in school, in the community, and in life.

  • Develop academic competence and critical thinking.
  • Build confidence, independence, and creative expression.
  • Instil strong moral values grounded in faith.
  • Foster community, collaboration, and empathy.
Our Vision

To be a leading center of Montessori excellence — producing graduates who are academically strong, spiritually grounded, and equipped to make meaningful contributions to society.

  • Recognised quality in early childhood education.
  • A model school for character and academic achievement.
  • A community of families, teachers, and learners united in purpose.
  • Graduates who lead with integrity wherever they go.
